About Us Just The Four Of Us!

Simon

Simon was born in Nottingham on February 7th, 1958 to his mother Margaret Marriott and father Cliff Marriott. In his early years he lived in the district of Old Basford, and in August 1957 his sister, Cathryn was born. In September 1962, he started school at Heathfield Primary School, Scotland Road, Basford.

In June 1966, the family moved into a new house at Rise Park Estate, and this meant a change of school to Springfield Primary School located in Bulwell.

At the age of 10, in September 1968 he moved in to secondary education at the Ellis Guildford Bilateral School, Bar Lane, Nottingham.

Events During My Life

Here are just a couple:-

  1. The 1966 FIFA World Cup, the eighth staging of the World Cup, was held in England from 11 to 30 July. England beat West Germany 4–2 in the final, winning the Jules Rimet Trophy.
  2. Apollo 11 was the first spaceflight that landed humans on the Moon. Mission commander Neil Armstrong and pilot Buzz Aldrin landed the lunar module Eagle on July 20, 1969, at 20:18 UTC. Armstrong became the first to step onto the lunar surface six hours later on July 21 at 02:56:15 UTC; Aldrin joined him about 20 minutes later. They spent about two and a quarter hours together outside the spacecraft, and collected 47.5 pounds (21.5 kg) of lunar material for return to Earth. Michael Collins piloted the command module Columbia alone in lunar orbit while they were on the Moon's surface. Armstrong and Aldrin spent just under a day on the lunar surface before rendezvousing with Columbia in lunar orbit.

Sharon

Sharon was born in Derby on March 18th, 1970 to her mother Dawn Coyne and father Gerard Coyne. She has an elder brother Philip, and a younger brother Andrew and they all lived on the district of Spondon. In her earlier years, she attended the Asterdale Primary School. a few minutes walk from home.

At age 10 in September 1980, she moved in to secondary education where she attended the Spondon School, since re-built and renamed to West Park School.

On Saturday, August 12th 2000 Sharon Coyne Married Simon Marriott at St Werbergh's Church in Spondon, Derby. Sharon was given away by her older brother, Phil, standing in for her dad Gerry, who sadly passed away in 1989

Our wedding reception was located at a truly wonderful venue, Risley Hall Country Park Hotel & Spa, Derby. Upon arrival we were served Champagne and, of course, pots of Tea in the spectacular gardens, where our guests mingled and chatted in the glorious sun shine. After an hour or so, we were called to the reception hall where we greeted our guests, before sitting down to a hearty meal. In the evening we had a buffet tea and disco and additional guests arrived. We stayed over in the Bridal Suite with a King Size four poster bed.

On Monday 14th August, we had an early start when my Best Man drove us to Heathrow Airport for our long journey to our Honeymoon destination of Hawaii.

Since moving to Yaxley in 2001, Sharon has worked at City Electrical Factors (CEF) and a family run business in Yaxley called Clark Drain.

Danielle

Danielle was born at 08:46 on 5th October 2011 at Peterborough City Hospital, weighing in at 6lb 4oz (2.835Kg). At six months old, she started swimming lessons at CalmaBaby and remained there until April 2016. Just before her 1st birthday, she started nursery and pre-school at Little Acorns Day Nursery and Montessori School

In August 2016, she started swimming again at Billie's Fins Swim School where teaching focusses more on technique, but she is loving it and we expect her to complete her Level One grade quite quickly.

Growing up fast, she started 'Big School' on Monday 12th September at Fourfields Community Primary School just a few minutes walk from home.
